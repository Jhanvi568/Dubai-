Cultural Norms

Dubai blends modernity with tradition, and understanding its cultural norms is essential for a respectful visit.

1. Respect for Religion

  • Islamic Practices: Islam is the official religion. Observe prayer times and be respectful during Ramadan, avoiding food and drink in public during daylight hours.

    • 2. Dress Code

  • Modesty: Dress modestly in public. Women should cover shoulders and knees, while men should avoid shorts above the knee, except at beaches and pools.

    • 3. Public Behavior

  • Social Etiquette: Public displays of affection are frowned upon. Handshakes are common, but wait for the other person to initiate, especially with women.
  • Alcohol:Consumed only in licensed venues; public intoxication is prohibited.

    • 4. Dining Etiquette

    Invitations:Remove shoes when entering a home. Accept food and drink when offered.

    5. Photography

    Privacy:Always ask permission before taking photos of people, especially women and children.

    Conclusion
