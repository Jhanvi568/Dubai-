Visa Requirements

1. Tourist Visa

  • Short-term Visa (30 days): Eligibility: Many nationalities can apply. Fee: Approximately 300 AED. Extension: Can be extended for an additional 30 days for about 600 AED.
  • Long-term Visa (90 days): Eligibility: Available for certain nationalities. Fee: Approximately 1,000 AED.

    • 2. Visa on Arrival

  • Eligibility: Citizens of select countries (e.g., USA, UK, Canada, EU members). Duration: Valid for up to 30 days.
  • Fee: Approximately 100 AED for a 30-day extension if needed.

    • 3. Transit Visa

  • Eligibility: For travelers transiting through Dubai for more than 8 hours but less than 96 hours.
  • Fee: Approximately 100 AED.
  • Requirements: Must have a confirmed onward ticket.

    • 4. Business Visa

  • Eligibility: Requires a local sponsor (usually a company in Dubai). Fee: Approximately 1,200 AED for a 30-day visa; longer durations may vary based on sponsorship.
  • Fee: Approximately 100 AED for a 30-day extension if needed.

    • 5. Residence Visa

  • Eligibility: For those planning to live in Dubai, usually tied to employment or family sponsorship.
  • Fee: Ranges from 3,000 AED to 7,000 AED depending on the duration and type of residency.

    • 6. Student Visa

    Eligibility: For students enrolled in accredited educational institutions in Dubai.

  • Fee: Approximately 1,200 AED annually, plus additional costs for medical insurance.

    • 7. Exemptions and Special Cases

    Some nationalities may have different requirements or exemptions based on bilateral agreements. Always check if your nationality qualifies for special conditions.

    Required Documents

  • Valid passport with at least six months’ validity.
  • Passport-sized photographs.
  • Proof of accommodation (hotel booking or invitation letter).
  • Proof of funds (bank statements).
  • Return flight ticket.

    • Application Process

    Applications can be submitted online, through travel agencies, or directly at UAE embassies and consulates. Processing times may vary from a few hours to several days.

    Conclusion

    Always check the latest regulations from official sources, as visa rules and fees can change. It’s advisable to prepare your application in advance to avoid any last-minute issues.
